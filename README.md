# The Taylor Series Of Heaven

**We can't make our world better but we can create imagine world that will live forever in our memory.**
I was desperate to see that our real world is ruled by old grandpas who waste the precious lives of kids and young people for their ego. I hate the world where we live and fight to change it, but it often feels impossible. We are late to create utopia in reality.

We live in a world where stupidity often prevails over wisdom, where the richest and most powerful decide the destiny of kind and ordinary people. A world where 1% of the population can spend more on breakfast than my parents make in a month. A world where islands are created for exploitation and human lives are treated like toys.

I refuse to believe that this is the only world we deserve.

Sometimes I feel like Don Quixote, fighting windmills that will never fall. In times like these, people want to hide somewhere safe — a place where ideas, justice, and imagination still matter.

This repository is my attempt to create that place.

Initially, I planned to write this book alone. I already made some progress with my previous novel:

https://www.amazon.com/-/zh_TW/Mykola-Klymenko-ebook/dp/B0DR3MVQTG

If you want to understand the philosophy and context behind this project, it is **highly recommended to read that novel first**.

But this new world does not belong only to me.
It can grow with the ideas of others.

Together we can build a world **they cannot steal from us**.

# What This Book Is About

**Taylor Series** is a philosophical and fictional exploration of a world where **Heaven and Hell exist as real destinations**.

In this universe:

* The afterlife is not a mystery.
* Society adapts to the knowledge of Heaven and Hell.
* Systems, rules, and even economies evolve around salvation and morality.
* Humanity tries to optimize eternity the same way it optimizes everything else.

Just like a Taylor series in mathematics approximates a function through many small terms, human life is built from countless choices that gradually reveal the final result.

Different lives.
Different paths.
One convergence.

# How You Can Contribute

This project is open for creative contributions.

You are welcome to:

* propose new ideas for the world
* introduce new characters
* expand storylines
* improve the narrative
* add philosophical reflections
* create maps or visualizations of the world

However, all contributions must remain **consistent with the main scenario and philosophy of the book**.

All pull requests are **reviewed and approved by the author** before being merged.

Contributors whose work becomes part of the story may be **credited as co-authors or contributors**, but **all rights and royalties of the book remain with the author**.

Please read **CONTRIBUTING.md** before submitting changes.

---

# How to Contribute (Pull Request Guide)

1. Fork this repository.
2. Create a branch for your idea or change.
3. Make your edits or additions.
4. Submit a **Pull Request** explaining:

   * what you added
   * how it fits into the world
   * which part of the story it expands

Your contribution will be reviewed and may be:

* accepted
* modified
* discussed
* or declined if it conflicts with the story.
