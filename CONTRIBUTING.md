# Contributing to *The Taylor Series Of Heaven*

Thank you for your interest in contributing to **The Taylor Series Of Heaven**.

This project is an open collaborative book. Readers are welcome to suggest ideas, improvements, and even new characters or story elements. Contributions help expand the world and perspectives represented in the book.

## How to Contribute

You are welcome to:

* Suggest edits or improvements to existing chapters
* Propose new scenes or story ideas
* Introduce new characters (personages)
* Improve language, clarity, or structure
* Fix grammar or formatting

To contribute:

1. Fork the repository.
2. Make your changes in your fork.
3. Submit a **Pull Request** describing your contribution.

All contributions should remain **consistent with the main storyline and world of the book**.

## Review Process

All pull requests are reviewed by the author.

The author may:

* accept the contribution
* request changes
* adapt or modify the contribution before merging
* decline the contribution if it conflicts with the story direction

Final editorial decisions belong to the author.

## Authorship and Credit

Contributors whose work is accepted may be credited as **co-authors or contributors** in the book.

However, contributors **do not obtain ownership rights to the book**.

## Contributor Agreement

By submitting a pull request to this repository, you agree that:

* You grant the author, **Mykola Klymenko**, a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, modify, publish, and distribute your contribution as part of the book *The Taylor Series Of Heaven*.
* The author retains **full copyright, editorial control, and all commercial rights** to the book and any future editions or derivative works.
* The author may publish the book commercially and receive royalties.
* Contributors will not claim ownership or financial rights to the book.

## Be Respectful

Please keep discussions constructive and respectful. This project welcomes thoughtful ideas and creative perspectives.
